Changelog

V1.0 - site released in state ready for public use, containing the following resources:

  • Elsword:
    • Boss guides preserved from the Elsword forum on Skin Splitter, Varnimyr Raids, and Master Road. Slight updates and corrections were made to Ventus' and Denif's guides, as well as the Varnimyr Raid introduction and Crimson Cradle guide.
    • Boss guides on El's Aspiration and Herbaon. These include video guides as well as text guides (El's Aspiration has a completely new guide; Herbaon's guide was not preserved from the forum, and had to be rewritten).
  • Genshin Impact:
    • A task checklist for daily and weekly activities
    • A wish simulator
  • Etterna:
    • A tool to determine the time between beats, given a BPM and beat snap
    • A set of links containing resources that may be useful to charters or players seeking to learn or improve their 4-key gameplay
